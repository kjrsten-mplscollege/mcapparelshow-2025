Lizeth Lema Puma

Eve

Model Michelle Contreras wears a full-circle bridal dress made from Seda Mandalay Marfil, a fabric that provides great comfort, drapability, and a smooth hand.

This look features a pleated organza shrug that complements the dress. The pleats are accompanied by pearlescent beads that give a soft touch and charming detail.

Michelle poses to show the V neckline at the back of the dress, highlighting the dress’s simple elegance.

Designer Bio

Lizeth grew up in Cuenca, Ecuador. At twelve, she developed an interest in fashion and was inspired by Selena Quintanilla. Four years later, at sixteen, she made a newspaper dress for her art class during her senior year of high school. In this project, she experimented with volume and floral origami, elements that can be seen in her collection “Eve.” This project gave her the confidence and motivation to come to Minneapolis College to pursue their Apparel Technologies program.

Designer Statement

As my first collection, “Eve” is inspired by the first woman on earth, whom I see as a muse. To me, Eve is intelligent, rational, ambitious, and brave, despite her being described as and created to be Adam’s companion. The concepts of power, force, and union influenced my designs. With my collection I hope to inspire women and make them feel secure, comfortable, fearless, and powerful in their own skin and in the clothes they decide to wear.
