Abigail Gilbertson

Gawk

Every piece is crafted with a focus on luxury, using premium materials like black lace, satin, and silk to create garments that embrace the body with softness and elegance.

Black serves as the core of the collection’s palette, its timeless sophistication enhanced by the rich textures of lace and silks. Carefully selecting these fabrics to drape and shape the body in ways that feel indulgent yet effortless.

Special attention was given to details like fit and texture to ensure that every piece feels as elegant as it looks. With refined construction and meticulously placed seams, each design was made to move with the body.

Designer Bio

Abigail Gilbertson is a lingerie designer on a mission to change how women see themselves in the mirror. Raised in the Midwest and studied at MCTC, she designs for the woman who refuses to shrink, offering undergarments that fit, flatter, and empower. Her pieces are rooted in inclusivity and self-celebration, with sizing and styling that challenge outdated beauty norms. Her line is designed for women of all shapes and backgrounds, with the goal of replacing shame with celebration. Fueled by her own journey of self-acceptance and a passion for inclusive design, Abigail is redefining the standards of beauty one bralette at a time. Whether it’s lace, mesh, or minimalist silhouettes, her designs are made for real bodies and real confidence. As she continues her education at the University of Minnesota, Abigail is focused on building a brand that uplifts, rather than idealizes. Her work is a love letter to women everywhere soft, strong, and entirely unapologetic.

Collection Overview

The collection is composed of five distinct yet cohesive looks, each exuding a sultry, romantic feel. This balance of romance and edge creates a unique aesthetic that feels timeless yet modern. Featuring dresses, bras, corsets, undies, bloomers, mini skirts, stockings, and garters, the collection offers something for every lingerie lover. The silhouettes are designed to accentuate natural beauty while offering just the right amount of coverage, making the pieces as practical as they are alluring. The soul of this collection lies in its seamless blend of comfort and sensuality. Designed with the modern woman in mind, the pieces celebrate individuality, confidence, and self-love. At its core, the collection conveys a powerful message that true beauty comes from being comfortable in your own skin. From conception to creation, the design process revolved around a single idea that lingerie should not only look stunning but also feel incredible to wear. By focusing on comfort alongside aesthetics, the collection stands out as a celebration of self-expression and self-acceptance, encouraging wearers to embrace their own beauty and individuality.
