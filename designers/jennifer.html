Designer Bio

Jennifer Cruz, a California-born designer, is the creative force behind the "Amador" collection, a showcase of richly embellished special occasion dresses and impeccably tailored men's suits, and handcrafted leather purses and bags all brought to life from her own meticulously created patterns. While possessing a lifelong creative inclination, her journey into design truly began with sewing at the age of 14. The name "Amador," meaning "to love," reflects her deep affection for the craft, poured into every stitch of this collection inspired by the visual narratives of movies and television, where each opulent and timeless dress unfolds a distinct and captivating story. This inaugural fashion show marks an exciting first step in what promises to be a lasting career.

Collection Overview

A collection born from my own designs, featuring elegant dresses with delicate beading and one uniquely custom-tailored suit. From the initial sketch to the final bead and stitch, each piece in the "Amador" collection reflects a personal journey of creation. Explore dresses with flowing lines and exquisite hand-beading, alongside a meticulously crafted, custom-tailored suit, all stemming from my own original patterns. "Amador," the Spanish word for " to love," signifies the heartfelt connection between creator and creation.
