“Life After Love”

Designer Bio

Veronica comes from a “hands on” family that cultivated a deep appreciation for the arts. Her interest in clothes started at a young age, inspired by her mom’s work as a seamstress in her hometown of Kiev, Ukraine. After debuting her first collection and graduating from the Minneapolis College Apparel Technologies program, Veronica plans to continue her education at the University of Minnesota, pursuing a bachelor's degree in Apparel Design. Veronica hopes to spend some time studying abroad as well.

Designer Statement

My debut collection, “Life After Love,” is an homage to womanhood and the return to oneself after a period of feeling stuck. My goal with “Life After Love” is to empower the wearer with elegantly sensual silhouettes and fabrics. A neutral color story showcases the beauty of simplicity, with additions of color as representations of spontaneity and the importance of a little fun. The inspiration came to me as I was personally experiencing the process of learning to prioritize loving myself after so many years of prioritizing romantic love. “Life After Love” is a collection of the feelings I never got to express and the love I should’ve given myself.
