Designer Bio

Mason Abbas is an American fashion designer redefining everyday wear to empower and inspire a new sense of personal style. Studying fashion design technologies at Minneapolis College, his designs are characterized by clean silhouettes, timeless structures, and preppy aesthetics for a uniform that feels as unique as its host. His creations are influenced by the likes of Virgil Abloh and Thom Browne, culminating in elevated streetwear styles that are effortlessly cool. After spending some time in the industry and advancing his skills with personal projects, Mason would like to operate and design for his own label.

Collection Overview

Unique yet uniform, “Ominosus” is a timeless collection for the modern minimalist. Combining clean lines and preppy aesthetics, this collection redesigns everyday wear through elevated streetwear styles that are classy and effortless.
With a neutral color palette, featuring classic black, white, grey, and beige, it harnesses smart silhouettes for comfortable designs that are always in style. Tailored jackets, sweater vests, and classic trousers and skirt styles deliver subtle sophistication to inspire personal style with simplicity and ease.
