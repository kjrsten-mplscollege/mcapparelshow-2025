Designer Bio

Esmeralda Andrade is a bold and visionary fashion designer whose brand, Regal, embodies the elegance, strength, and sophistication of her Dominican and Ecuadorian roots. Born in New York and raised in Cuenca, Ecuador, she fuses Caribbean playfulness with refined design to create pieces that are clean, powerful, and full of personality. Represented by the color black in all its forms and textures, Regal is a celebration of boldness and clarity. As a devoted mother, Esmeralda infuses her work with the love, resilience, and depth that motherhood has taught her. Fashion is her way of life—she breathes it daily, dressing as if every day were a red carpet to inspire herself and those around her. Follow her journey on Instagram at @esmeandradelopez and her brand at @regalbyEsmeralda.

Collection Overview

Regal is a bold and powerful collection born from the depths of personal vision and unapologetic self-expression. Inspired by the timeless elegance and mystery of black, Regal embodies a fearless femininity through daring silhouettes, luxurious textures, and striking contrasts. From supple leather and plush fur to sleek metal accents. Every piece is a manifestation of what the designer wished existed: garments that celebrate the strength, sensuality, and beauty of women without compromise. Comfortable yet captivating, sexy yet sophisticated, Regal is not just fashion, it’s an attitude. With its clean lines, commanding presence, and artful detail, this collection speaks to the woman who leads, who owns her power, and who dresses not for approval but to express her inner royalty.
