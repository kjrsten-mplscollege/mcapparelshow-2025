Designer Bio

Najmo was born in Somalia, but she was raised in Kenya Nairobi, and she come from a vibrant community that inspires creativity in every corner. Her design style is an eclectic mix of contemporary aesthetics and traditional influences, characterized by bold colors and intricate patterns. Through her work, she tells stories that connect people to their roots while embracing modernity. Her passion for design began in her childhood, thanks to her mother, who owned a salon. She spent countless hours helping her, which ignited her love for aesthetics and the transformative power of beauty. She often found herself experimenting with colors and styles, creating art frames that reflected her imaginative spirit. These early experiences shaped her creative identity and instilled in herself love for fashion that goes beyond clothing; it’s about expression and empowerment. What sets me apart in the fashion world is my commitment to authenticity and storytelling. She believes every piece should carry a narrative, reflecting the wearer’s journey and identity. She embraces sustainable practices, focusing on ethical sourcing and production methods that honor both the environment and the artisans behind her creations. My approach is rooted in inclusivity, aiming to create designs that resonate with diverse audiences. Throughout her career, she had worked with bridals where her work has received enthusiastic acclaim. Her education in fashion design has provided her with a solid foundation, and she enjoyed collaborating with fellow artists, which has broadened her creative horizons. Notable milestones. At the heart of her work is a desire to connect with her audience on a personal level. She draws inspiration from everyday life, infusing her designs with elements that resonate with shared experiences. Her journey is about fostering a sense of community through creativity. She strives to create pieces that evoke nostalgia, sparking joy and confidence in those who wear them. Looking ahead, she is excited about the future of her artistry. She hopes to expand her brand to include more sustainable practices and explore new collaborations with like-minded creatives. She envisions a world where fashion serves as a bridge, connecting people across cultures and backgrounds. She aspiration is to continue evolving as an artist, inspiring others while creating designs that resonate deeply within the hearts of her client’s.

Collection Overview

"Indho Uroon" is a captivating collection that embodies the essence of cultural heritage blended with contemporary aesthetics. Each piece tells a story, showcasing intricate craftsmanship and vibrant colors that reflect the richness of tradition while appealing to modern sensibilities. The designs in "Indho Uroon" celebrate individuality and elegance, offering a range of garments that are both striking and versatile. With flowing silhouettes and detailed embellishments, this collection invites wearers to embrace their unique style and express their personality, making every moment a celebration of beauty and grace.
