Bonne Ogbu

Kijie

Model Tabitha is wearing a one shoulder, princess cut dress with a long train that has been draped around the dress. The train is made to be long and has a drape with an idea of going with the wind. The dress itself is a princess line, sweetheart neckline and the drape is done over one shoulder.

The side of the dress is open, giving a view of the fabric design coming together to give a beautiful symmetrical view and also showing what the dress looks like on the inside.

The drape is done making pleats from the shoulder to the waist making the drape to relax also revealing a beautiful back silhouette. The head piece is called “Gele” - a Nigerian headpiece worn with traditional attires.

Designer Bio

Bonne Ogbu is a Nigerian born designer who has a love and passion for clothes and accessories. Born to Nigerian parents, and spent most of her life (so far), in her home country Nigeria, she hopes to bring a mix of culture and fashion, hence the name of her Brand “Buseh by Bonneh”. When you think of her brand, you think of elegance, comfort, a brand that brings out the inner beauty of women, and a brand that represents Christian values. She is inspired by nature, cultures, colors and what they represent. Although she started with Alterations, In the long term she hopes to add joy to beautiful days by designing and styling for intending brides, all things bridals from dresses to accessories. It is her goal to design from runways, to wedding aisles, to red carpet events. Wisdom is also a believer of Christ and the way he showed us to love, and this is a core value of her life and business.

Collection Overview

Kijie the collection, celebrates womanhood and beauty. The beauty of love whether its loving oneself, another, beauty in others, the beauty of nature and a splash of culture. The colors purple, silver and white representing royalty, wealth, fertility, grace, elegance. The mix of fabric showing a fusion of African culture and style. Buseh by Bonneh is a brand that believes every woman is a noble queen in her own beauty and strength, carrying the God given power of Fertility, perfection and wealth with grace, elegance and glamor, and we want the world to see it too, one piece at a time.
