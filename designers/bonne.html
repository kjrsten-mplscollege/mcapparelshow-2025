Bonne Ogbu

Kijie

Every piece is crafted with a focus on luxury, using premium materials like black lace, satin, and silk to create garments that embrace the body with softness and elegance.

Black serves as the core of the collection’s palette, its timeless sophistication enhanced by the rich textures of lace and silks. Carefully selecting these fabrics to drape and shape the body in ways that feel indulgent yet effortless.

Special attention was given to details like fit and texture to ensure that every piece feels as elegant as it looks. With refined construction and meticulously placed seams, each design was made to move with the body.

Designer Bio

Bonne Ogbu is a Nigerian born designer who has a love and passion for clothes and accessories. Born to Nigerian parents, and spent most of her life (so far), in her home country Nigeria, she hopes to bring a mix of culture and fashion, hence the name of her Brand “Buseh by Bonneh”. When you think of her brand, you think of elegance, comfort, a brand that brings out the inner beauty of women, and a brand that represents Christian values. She is inspired by nature, cultures, colors and what they represent. Although she started with Alterations, In the long term she hopes to add joy to beautiful days by designing and styling for intending brides, all things bridals from dresses to accessories. It is her goal to design from runways, to wedding aisles, to red carpet events. Wisdom is also a believer of Christ and the way he showed us to love, and this is a core value of her life and business.

Collection Overview

Kijie the collection, celebrates womanhood and beauty. The beauty of love whether its loving oneself, another, beauty in others, the beauty of nature and a splash of culture. The colors purple, silver and white representing royalty, wealth, fertility, grace, elegance. The mix of fabric showing a fusion of African culture and style. BusehbyBonneh is a brand that believes every woman is a noble queen in her own beauty and strength, carrying the God given power of Fertility, perfection and wealth with grace, elegance and glamor, and we want the world to see it too, one piece at a time.
